FIRST - Infinite Recharge Challenge

Overview of the Game

The Infinite Recharge Challenge is a robotics competition organized by FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology). The goal of the game is to build a robot that can score points by shooting power cells into a target, climbing a tower, and defending the tower from opposing robots.
